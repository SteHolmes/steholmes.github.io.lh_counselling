What is Counselling?

Counselling is a collaborative talking therapy that gives you a safe space to explore some of your most inner thoughts and feelings. You will be greeted in a warm nonjudgmental way, and with the freedom to talk about anything you choose in a confidential setting.

Sometimes the counsellor will just listen to you, and other times they will offer their thoughts and responses. This will be done in a kind and sensitive manner, to help you explore how you may be feeling in the present or in regards to a past situation.

The counselling process can be transformative and may help you in an immediate crisis or to make sense of troubling events from the past .

Why Choose Counselling?

How often in life are we truly listened to without judgment or opinion? This simple act alone can be incredibly powerful.

Do you feel completely misunderstood and wish you could explore the reasons why?

Do you feel in constant emotional pain?

Do you feel haunted by the past?

Do you feel trapped by addiction or self-harm?

Are you tired of toxic relationships or repeating the same mistakes over and over again?

Do you feel anxious, depressed, confused or full of self doubt?

Would you like to get some clarity on why you feel the way you do, understand yourself better and begin a path to healing and happiness?

I can not fix you or instantly take away your pain, but will help you to put pieces of your jigsaw together and begin the path to healing and happiness.
